2017 CFP National Championship Story

Here is the story of the 2017 CFP National Championship:

The Matchup

Clemson came into the game ranked #2 in the nation. They had a couple of scares during the season, namely a game against NC State that went to overtime only because NC State missed a 33 yard field goal. Because of this, many did not believe they belonged here. Alabama came into this game ranked #1 in the nation. Most people even said they were the best college football team of all time as they dominated every opponent they faced. They were the clear favorite.

The Game

Alabama came out of gate rolling. Clemson received the kickoff, but even still Alabama went up 14-0 early in the 2nd quarter. Clemson's QB Deshaun Watson put the team on his back as he managed a tough 8 yard scramble into the end zone to cut the lead to 14-7. However, after a costly interception thrown by Watson, Alabama grew the lead to 17-7. Clemson responded with a TD to bring the game back to 17-14. However, Alabama reminded the world why they were ranked the best team in the nation as they grew the lead to 24-14 with a TD of their own. Clemson responded with a TD to drop the lead to 3. Clemson then got their first lead of the game with another TD late in the game making the game 28-24. Alabama then crushed the hearts of Clemson fans as their QB Jalen Hurts managed to muster a 30 yard rushing TD with only 2 minutes left. Then came one of the greatest drives in all of college football. The Watson led Tigers marched down to the 2 yard line, down by 3 with only 6 seconds left. On what would be the last play of the game, Deshaun Watson threw a game winning TD pass to Hunter Renfrow, winning the National Championship game.

Final Game Stats

  • Clemson ended the game with 511 total yards. 420 threw the air and ony 91 on the ground.
  • Alabama ended the game with 376 total yards. 155 threw the air and 221 on the ground.
